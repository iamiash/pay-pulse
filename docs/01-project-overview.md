PayPulse is a personal web-based app made to help students track their digital subscriptions and free trial deadlines in one easy dashboard. 

These days, almost everyone uses multiple streaming platforms, premium learning tools, or developer utilities (like Spotify, Netflix, Canva, or GitHub Copilot). Managing all these recurring monthly or yearly fees can get really confusing. A lot of apps offer short free trials but require card details upfront, which means people often get charged automatically if they forget to cancel on time. 

PayPulse will solve this by letting users manually list their active subscriptions, view upcoming payment deadlines, track their total monthly expenses, and see clear alerts before a free trial ends.
