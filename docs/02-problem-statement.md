The main issue this project solves is losing money on forgotten digital subscriptions and automated free trial charges.

Main Problems:
1. The Free Trial Trap: Apps make it super simple to sign up for a free trial but count on users forgetting to cancel before the automatic payment kicks in. Students lose money on services they only needed for a short project.
2. Multi-Currency Issues: A lot of platforms charge in foreign currency (like USD). With bank card conversion rates constantly shifting, it is hard to know exactly how much local currency is actually leaving your account.
3. No Simple Tracking System: Right now, students either try to make confusing spreadsheets that require manual updates, scroll through chaotic email receipts, or just wait for a sudden SMS alert from their bank after the money has already been deducted.
