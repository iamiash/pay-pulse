Since PayPulse is a standalone utility app built by an individual student developer, the stakeholders involved are direct and simple:

Primary Users (Students & Young Professionals): They are the core focus. They need a quick, simple way to log their subscriptions manually without going through a complicated or unsafe setup process.
The Developer (Me): Responsible for designing the frontend dashboard UI, managing the local storage logic, and ensuring the codebase remains manageable for a single semester project.
Academic Evaluators (Faculty & Lab Instructors): They need to see that the project follows proper software engineering processes, organized requirements documentation, and clear individual Git workflows.
