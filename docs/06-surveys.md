I ran a quick, anonymous poll among some students to get an idea of typical subscription habits.

Survey Data Points:
Subscription Count: 80% of students surveyed manage between 2 to 5 active digital subscriptions at the same time (including shared accounts).
Forgetfulness: 11 out of 15 students admitted they have lost money in the past year because they forgot to cancel a trial before the automatic billing cycle started.
Current Methods: 70% said they use absolutely no active tracking system, while the other 30% just write a quick list in their phone's default notes app.
